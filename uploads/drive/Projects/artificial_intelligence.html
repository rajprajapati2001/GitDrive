4. Neural Network Mechanics

Deep systems evaluate variables by chaining structural blocks together that perform geometric calculations over interconnected array structures.

$$y = f(\mathbf{W} \cdot \mathbf{x} + \mathbf{b})$$
Where $\mathbf{W}$ represents the synaptic weight matrix, $\mathbf{x}$ is the input vector, $\mathbf{b}$ is the bias offset, and $f$ scales outputs through non-linear activation functions.

Using an optimization algorithm called backpropagation, errors flow in reverse direction through network branches to systematically tweak internal node coefficients and minimize total prediction error.

5. The Transformer Architecture Shift
